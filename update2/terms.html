You may want to consult a lawyer to understand the extent of legal protection of your genetic information before you share it with anybody. Some states have laws that protect individuals with regard to their genetic information. Furthermore, genetic information that you choose to share with your physician or other health care provider may become part of your medical record and could become accessible to other health care providers and/or insurance companies in the future. As new discoveries are made, the meaning of your Genetic Information could prove different in the future than it does today.
