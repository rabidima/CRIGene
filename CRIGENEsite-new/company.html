About Us

Our Founder: CRI Genetics is led by Alexei Fedorov, a molecular geneticist with over 35 years of experience studying DNA. A former apprentice of Nobel Prize winning scientist Walter Gilbert, Alexei has created a patented DNA analysis algorithm using the latest breakthroughs in genetic science.

Alexei’s Vision: Alexei Fedorov’s lifetime goal has been to advance Mankind’s understanding of the human genome into new frontiers. His vision for CRI Genetics is to help you use your DNA to get to know yourself in the most personal way possible. There is a treasure trove of secrets hidden in your genetic code. These secrets can not only give you insight into your ancestry, but can even help you make decisions that lead to a happier, healthier life. That is the mission of CRI Genetics.

What Makes Us Different: Our reports are based on actual genetic research. As a professional geneticist who has authored several projects, Alexei Fedorov is at the forefront of the latest advancements in genetic science. CRI Genetics uses his expertise to deliver you information that you can use to raise your understanding of yourself to a higher level.
